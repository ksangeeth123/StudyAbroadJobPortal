Inquiry Handling Specialist

Study Abroad Private Limited üìç On-site Position

About Us

We are a dynamic study abroad agency dedicated to helping students pursue higher education overseas. We connect applicants with prospective programs, provide guidance through the application process, and support families every step of the way with 18+ years of service excellence.

Role Overview

We are seeking a motivated and articulate female Inquiry Handling Specialist who will be the first point of contact for prospective students and their families. Your primary responsibilities will be to call inquiries, qualify and filter positive leads, and hand off high-potential prospects to the Marketing team for further engagement.

Responsibilities

  • Make outbound and handle inbound calls to prospective students and parents in a courteous, professional, and informative manner.
  • Qualify inquiries by assessing interest, eligibility, timelines, budget, and program fit.
  • Filter and categorize inquiries, prioritizing high-potential leads for the Marketing team.
  • Maintain accurate records of all interactions in the CRM, including call notes and next steps.
  • Provide timely follow-ups and coordinate with Marketing for targeted outreach.
  • Meet and maintain key performance indicators (KPIs) such as call volume, conversion rate, and data accuracy.
  • Collaborate with other teams (Counseling, Admissions, Marketing) to ensure a seamless student journey.
  • Uphold company values and maintain a positive, customer-centric approach.

Requirements

  • Female candidate preferred.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English.
  • Proficiency in Sinhala and/or Tamil is a strong asset.
  • Strong phone etiquette, active listening, and compelling conversational skills.
  • Good organizational skills with attention to detail.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team; results-driven mindset.
  • Prior experience in inbound/outbound calling, customer service, or education consulting is a plus.
  • A degree or diploma preferred, not mandatory.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and performance-based incentives.
  • Comprehensive training and ongoing professional development.
  • Opportunities for career growth within sales, marketing, and student advisory tracks.
  • Friendly, supportive team culture.

How to Apply

Please submit:

  • Your updated CV/resume
  • Any relevant certifications or past experience in study abroad or education services

Email applications to: info@studyabroad.lk

Application deadline: 5th December 2025
